    AppendixMethodology & sources

    Source data. Every figure on this site comes from Tom Leatherwood's campaign financial disclosure reports filed with the Tennessee Registry of Election Finance (committee ID 7320), exported directly from the state's public database at apps.tn.gov/tncamp. Thirty reports, covering 3rd Quarter 2018 through 1st Quarter 2026. The ledger reconciles: every report's ending balance matches the next report's beginning balance, and the career math closes to the penny.

    Classification. Contributions are bucketed using TREF's own contributor flags from the filings: "P" designates a PAC, "C" designates a candidate committee. Business entities without a flag (LLCs, corporations, funds) are grouped with special interests. Campaign committees and sitting elected officials donating personally are grouped with politicians. Everyone else is an individual. All 390 checks are listed above so any single classification can be checked.

    Election cycles. TREF assigns each report to an election cycle; supplemental reports filed in odd years belong to the following election's cycle. "2026 cycle" means all filings the Registry assigns to the 2026 election: the 2025 supplementals and 2026 quarterlies filed to date.

    Corrections. Found an error? Every claim traces to a specific filing. Pull the report, check the math, and if something's wrong, it gets fixed.
